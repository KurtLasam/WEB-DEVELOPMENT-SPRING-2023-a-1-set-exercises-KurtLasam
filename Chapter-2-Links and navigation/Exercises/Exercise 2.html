ABOUT

The Flash is an American superhero television series developed by Greg Berlanti and Geoff Johns, The show is based on DC Comics "The Flash" and it is well knowned around the world, The Series premiered in the United States on The CW on October 7, 2014.

PLOT

Barry Allen was a criminal investigator for a local police department, when the particle exelerator exploded many of the people in the city was affected by the radition, but Barry Allen was struck by lightning cause of the weather change when the explosion happened, When we was struck it gave him Meta Powers and the ability to run fast like the speed of light, and with the help of his team The Team Flash they help criminals that have meta powers aswell.
